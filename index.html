Step 01

Determining visa type

The first step is to determine the appropriate visa type based on the purpose of the trip, such as tourist, business, student, work, or family visit.

Step 02

Submission of application

The applicant must submit the completed application form and supporting documents to the respective embassy, consulate, or visa application center of the destination.span>

Step 03

Biometrics and interviews

In some cases, applicants may need to undergo biometric data collection (fingerprinting) or attend an interview at the visa processing center.

Step 04

Decision and notification
